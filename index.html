The Stat Blueprint

A long-distance front-runner needs a deep well of Stamina to maintain the lead and high Speed to establish it. This build leverages Rice Shower's natural +20% Stamina and +10% Speed growth rates. Power is essential for acceleration out of corners and holding position.

The Training Timeline

Junior Year

Focus entirely on raising Support Card friendship gauges. Train where multiple supports are grouped to prepare for powerful "friendship training" later.

Classic Year

Begin targeting stats. Aim for 450-500 Speed & Stamina by the end of the year. Acquire `Swinging Maestro` as soon as possible. Utilize Summer Camp for major stat boosts.

Senior Year

Final push to reach the 800+ Speed/Stamina goals. Continue prioritizing rainbow friendship training. Fill out remaining useful skills before the final races.

URA Finals

With high stats and a key recovery skill, Rice Shower is now prepared to dominate from the front and secure a decisive victory.
